Nouns and Verbs

Nomino na Kitenzi (no-me-no na key-ten-zee)

Nouns (nomino)

Nouns are words that names somethings,place/somewhere or Someone(Nomino ni maneno jina ya kitu , pahali au Mtu)


  • Names(Jina)-Randy
  • Places(maeneo)-Nairobi
  • Things(Vitu)-Book(Kitabu)
  • Animals()-Lion(Simba)

Verds (kitenzi)

A word used to describe an action, state, or occurrence, and forming the main part of the predicate of a sentence, such as hear, become, happen. (Vitenzi ni maneno yanayosimamia kitendo.)

Adjectives

Kivumshi (key-vu-me-she)

Kivumishi

An adjective is a word that describes or modifies a noun or pronoun, providing more information about its qualities like size, color, or feeling.
